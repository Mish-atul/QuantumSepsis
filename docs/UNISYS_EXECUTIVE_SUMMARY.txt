================================================================================
THE PROBLEM
================================================================================

Sepsis kills 11 MILLION people per year globally. Every hour of delay in 
treatment increases mortality by ~7%. Current detection methods are REACTIVE, 
not PREDICTIVE. We need AI that warns clinicians 3-4 hours BEFORE clinical onset.

================================================================================
OUR SOLUTION
================================================================================

QuantumSepsis Shield: 5-layer hybrid quantum-classical AI pipeline

Layer 1: Classical Deep Learning (BiLSTM + Attention)
Layer 2: Quantum Kernel Methods (8-qubit ZZFeatureMap + SVM)
Layer 3: Quantum-Calibrated Conformal Prediction (QCCP)
Layer 4: Adversarial Safety Agents (Red Team Tripwires)
Layer 5: Confidence-Gated Orchestrator (Clinical Decision)

================================================================================
QUANTUM ADVANTAGE — THE KEY DIFFERENTIATOR
================================================================================

55.8% TIGHTER UNCERTAINTY INTERVALS using Quantum-Calibrated Conformal 
Prediction (QCCP) compared to classical conformal prediction.

Standard Conformal:  q_alpha = 1.0000, mean width = 1.0000
QCCP (Quantum):      q_alpha = 0.4398, mean width = 0.4420
Width Reduction:     55.8%

CLINICAL IMPACT: Tighter intervals = higher confidence = more aggressive 
early intervention for high-risk patients = lives saved.

================================================================================
QUANTUM COMPUTING IMPLEMENTATION
================================================================================

Platform:        IBM Qiskit AerSimulator
Qubits:          8
Feature Map:     ZZFeatureMap with linear entanglement
Repetitions:     2
Circuit Depth:   16 gates
Shots:           1,024 per circuit
Training Time:   41.4 minutes (500×500 kernel matrix)
Support Vectors: 395/500 (79% efficiency)

WHY QUANTUM? Classical kernels operate in explicit feature space. Quantum 
kernels map to exponentially large Hilbert space (2^8 = 256 dimensions), 
capturing non-linear correlations that classical methods miss.

Safety Features:

- Conformal Coverage: 99.46% (exceeds 90% statistical guarantee)

5 Clinical Tripwires (Non-Overridable):
1. Temperature: <36°C or >38.3°C
2. Heart Rate: >90 bpm with upward trend
3. Respiratory Rate: >20 breaths/min
4. Mean Arterial Pressure: <70 mmHg
5. Glasgow Coma Scale: <14

================================================================================
THREE NOVEL CONTRIBUTIONS
================================================================================

N1: Quantum-Calibrated Conformal Prediction (QCCP)
    Uses quantum kernel distance to sepsis centroids as nonconformity score
    Result: 55.8% tighter intervals with maintained coverage guarantee

N2: Adversarial Tripwire-Gated Asymmetric Safety
    Non-overridable clinical tripwires + adaptive loss doubling on near-misses
    Result: 49.9% of alerts include Red Team overrides (safety-first)

N3: Confidence-Gated Diagnostic Fast-Tracking
    High confidence (>80%) + high risk (>60%) → skip preliminary diagnostics
    Result: Reduces time-to-treatment by bypassing diagnostic queue
